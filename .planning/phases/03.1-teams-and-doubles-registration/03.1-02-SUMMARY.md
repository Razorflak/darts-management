## Decisions Made
- Applied `rawTx as unknown as postgres.Sql` cast in teams.ts — `TransactionSql<{}>` strips call signatures. Same pattern already established in `publish/+server.ts`.
- The `findOrCreate` pattern inside `sql.begin()` provides both atomicity and idempotency without requiring UPSERT syntax (which would require ON CONFLICT keys we don't have on `team`).
