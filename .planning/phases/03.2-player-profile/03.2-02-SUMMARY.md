## Decisions Made
- PlayerCreationForm renders fields-only (no form tag) so pages control the form element and can inject hidden inputs (redirectTo)
- licence_no guard enforced in both `load()` and `actions.default()` — protects against bypass via direct POST
- Schemas placed in event-schemas.ts (not request-schemas.ts) per plan — tightly coupled to Player domain type
